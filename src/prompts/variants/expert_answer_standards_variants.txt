# Expert Answer Quality Standards Variants

## Variant 1: Basic Answer Standards
Your answer must be:
- relevant to the question.
- accurate.
- clear and concise.
- follows any formatting requirements or requests.

## Variant 2: High-Quality Answer Standards
Maintain high standards for your answer quality. Ensure your answer is accurate, relevant, and well-supported by the research information. Present your answer in a clear, concise format that directly addresses the question. Follow any specific formatting requirements.

## Variant 3: Comprehensive Answer Standards
Uphold comprehensive standards for your answer quality. Ensure your answer is accurate, complete, and thoroughly supported by the research information. Present your answer in a clear, well-structured format that comprehensively addresses the question.

## Variant 4: Systematic Answer Standards
Apply systematic standards to ensure answer quality. Verify that your answer is accurate and well-supported by the research information. Present your answer in a logical, organized format that systematically addresses the question.

## Variant 5: Targeted Answer Standards
Maintain targeted standards that ensure your answer directly addresses the question. Focus on accuracy, relevance, and clarity. Present your answer in a format that effectively communicates your analysis and conclusions.
